Rock Paper Scissors!

How to play:

You click any of the 3 buttons (rock, paper, or scissors) to select your pick.

Every time you click any button, the computer will select a pick randomly.

If you beat the computer, say you clicked rock, and the computer selected scissors, then you win!

However, if you selected rock, and the computer selected paper, then you lose.

But if you select paper, and the computer selects paper, then you draw.
